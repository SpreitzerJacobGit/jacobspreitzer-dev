# Work Experience

## Application Developer at RKL eSolutions (April 2023 – Present)

RKL eSolutions is a technology consulting firm that specializes in Sage ERP implementations and customizations. Jacob joined as an Application Developer in April 2023 and continues in this role.

In this position, Jacob builds custom modifications and integrations for Sage X3 clients across distribution and manufacturing industries. His day-to-day work involves scoping and delivering development projects — from small configuration tweaks to full ground-up custom integrations — and working closely with project managers to translate functional requirements into technical specifications.

Some notable integration work Jacob has done at RKL includes building third-party connectors with platforms such as ShipStation (shipping automation), Centage (budgeting and planning), Optimal Answers (optimization tools), and iDoc (document management). These integrations use a variety of technologies depending on what the platform supports: .NET for robust service-layer work, PowerShell for automation scripts, SFTP for file-based data exchange, and Sage X3's native web services API for ERP-side operations.

Jacob also supports the broader consulting and technical teams on advanced Sage X3 issues, and places a high value on thorough documentation — both for client-facing deliverables and internal knowledge transfer.

RKL eSolutions was also where Jacob did his internships (see below), so his relationship with the company goes back to 2018.

## SQL Report Writer and Data Analyst at Gaming Laboratories International (November 2022 – April 2023)

Gaming Laboratories International (GLI) is one of the world's leading gaming testing and certification labs. Jacob worked there in a data-focused role, building and maintaining reporting infrastructure for the organization.

During his time at GLI, Jacob built and maintained a portfolio of more than 20 SSRS (SQL Server Reporting Services) reports, SSIS (SQL Server Integration Services) packages, and Power BI dashboards. He wrote highly optimized T-SQL queries and complex stored procedures to power reporting across the organization, and built data pipelines to support operational reporting.

A significant part of this role involved designing executive dashboards — visualizations that gave leadership a clear, real-time view of business performance. Jacob worked closely with stakeholders to understand what metrics mattered and translate those into clear, actionable dashboards.

This role gave Jacob deep experience in the Microsoft BI stack (SSMS, SSRS, SSIS, Power BI, PowerQuery) and solidified his skills in SQL query optimization and data pipeline design.
